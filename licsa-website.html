Wire the real signal

The ledger above runs on a heuristic engine, in your browser, matched against a curated life-insurance content taxonomy — no live network calls, no keys required. To replace it with live trend and competitor data, this is the appendix of sources built for that job.

Suggested wiring

Pull raw signal from Reddit + Google Trends (via SerpApi) + the Meta Ad Library for what competitors are actively running. Feed that raw material into the Claude API as the reasoning layer — it clusters the raw signal into themes, scores relevance to your segment, and drafts the content docket and campaign briefs in the same shape this page already produces. That keeps one model doing the intelligence work, and the APIs doing pure data-gathering.

LICSA — heuristic demo build. Not investment, insurance, or legal advice. Signal engine runs client-side. Nothing you paste is sent anywhere.
